FAQs

What is Fibra Internet?

Fibra Internet is a high-speed fiber-optic internet service designed for homes and businesses, offering reliable and fast connectivity.

How do I change my Wi-Fi password?

1. Open your router's admin panel (usually 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.0.1).
2. Log in with your admin credentials.
3. Go to the Wireless or Security settings.
4. Change the Wi-Fi password and save the settings.

What should I do if my internet is slow?

- Restart your router.
- Check for any ongoing outages in your area.
- Contact our support team for assistance.
